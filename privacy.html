Overview

This policy explains what happens to the information you give us when you use this website — for example when you request a consultation, sign up for our newsletter, or simply browse the site. We've written it to be genuinely readable, not just legally defensible.

This policy covers www.wisehomeopathy.com only. It does not yet cover any patient login, portal, or app — because none of those exist yet. See "Future update required" below for how that will change.

Information we collect

We only collect what you choose to give us, through one of these forms:

  • Consultation request forms (on the Contact page and the Blog page): your name, email, phone number, country, the condition you're asking about, and any message you write.
  • Newsletter signup (on the Blog page): your email address only.

We do not use a separate analytics or tracking tool on this website. The only automatic technical processing is what any website host inherently does to deliver pages to your browser securely — see "Cookies & third-party content" below.

Why we collect it

Solely to respond to you: to understand your enquiry, get back to you about booking a consultation, and — only if you opted in separately — send you our newsletter. We don't collect information for any other purpose.

How we use it

When you submit a form, it's delivered to our clinic team so we can read it and reply — by email, phone, or WhatsApp, whichever you've made available to us. Newsletter sign-ups are used only to send the newsletter you asked for.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not build profiles from it beyond what's needed to respond to your enquiry.

Who can see it

Your submission is seen by the Wise Homeopathy clinic team. Technically, form submissions pass through Netlify (our website hosting and forms provider) to reach us — Netlify processes this data on our behalf as our forms infrastructure, the same way any website's contact form relies on its hosting provider.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any other third party for marketing purposes. As of this policy's last update, no other service (no CRM, no advertising platform, no analytics vendor) receives the information you submit through these forms.

Cookies & third-party content

We keep this simple because our current setup is simple: this website does not use tracking or advertising cookies, and does not run any analytics script. That's why you won't see a cookie-consent banner here — we don't set the kind of cookies that would require one.

Two things on this site are loaded from Google's own servers, each governed by Google's own privacy policy rather than ours:

  • Google Fonts — used for the site's typography, on every page.
  • Google Maps — an embedded map on the Contact page showing our clinic location.

If you'd rather avoid any request to Google's servers, you can use browser extensions that block third-party embeds — the site will still function, just with a fallback system font and no interactive map.

How long we keep it

We don't yet have a formally defined retention schedule for form submissions — we're a small clinic team and this is an honest gap, not a hidden one. In practice, enquiry details are kept only as long as needed to respond to you and, if you become a patient, as part of your clinical record. If you'd like your enquiry details deleted, contact us (see below) and we'll do so.

How we protect it

This website is served entirely over HTTPS, and uses standard protective headers (including HSTS, X-Frame-Options, and a restrictive Permissions Policy) configured at the hosting level. As a static website with no database of our own, there is no separate application server or database for the public site to be attacked.

We do not currently hold a formal, published information-security certification (such as ISO 27001) for this website, and we won't claim one until it's genuinely true.

Your choices

  • You can ask us what information we hold about you.
  • You can ask us to correct information that's wrong.
  • You can ask us to delete your enquiry or newsletter details.
  • You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time by contacting us — every issue also reminds you that you're free to unsubscribe.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page. We don't yet have a self-service portal for this — you're reaching an actual person on our team.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children. Where a consultation concerns a minor, we expect the form to be submitted by a parent or guardian on the minor's behalf.

Other websites you may reach from here

This site links to WhatsApp, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Once you click through to any of them, you're on that platform, governed by its own privacy policy — not this one.

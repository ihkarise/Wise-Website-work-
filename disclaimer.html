This content is educational, not a diagnosis

Everything on this website — the condition pages, blog articles, FAQs, and Dr. Libin Job's writing — is provided for general education. None of it is a diagnosis, a prescription, or personal medical advice for your specific situation. Only an actual consultation, where a doctor reviews your full history, can offer that.

Homeopathy as complementary care

Our constitutional homeopathic approach is designed to work alongside your existing medical care, not to replace it. We never advise stopping prescribed medication without your treating physician's guidance, and we encourage continued monitoring with your specialist throughout treatment — this is consistent with how we describe our approach everywhere else on this website.

No guaranteed outcomes

Treatment outcomes vary by individual. We do not promise cures, and nothing on this website should be read as a guarantee of any particular result. What we can offer is an individualised, root-cause approach, transparently explained, with clear expectations set at your first consultation.

Not for medical emergencies

If you are experiencing a medical emergency

Contact your local emergency services or go to your nearest hospital immediately. Do not use this website, our contact forms, or WhatsApp for urgent or emergency symptoms — they are not monitored as an emergency channel.

Patient stories & testimonials

The patient stories on our homepage are shared with the patients' permission and describe their individual experience. They are not a guarantee that any other patient will have the same outcome — every case is different, which is the same reason our treatment approach is individualised rather than standardised.

Our doctors' credentials

You're welcome — and encouraged — to verify the qualifications of anyone treating you. Dr. Libin Job's credentials (BHMS, MD (Hom), TCMC registration) and the rest of our clinical team are listed on our Team page.

International patients

We offer online consultations to patients outside India, with remedies couriered internationally where applicable. Rules on receiving homeopathic remedies by post, and on telemedicine generally, vary by country. It's your responsibility to check what applies where you live; we're happy to discuss what we know about a specific destination when you get in touch.

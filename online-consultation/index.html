Frequently asked questions

Everything international patients ask before booking a consultation with Dr. Libin Job.

Can I consult Dr. Libin Job online from outside India?

Yes. We treat international patients by secure video consultation, with patients regularly joining from the UAE, UK, Canada, Ireland and Sri Lanka. Wherever you are, the process is the same: a detailed online case-taking session followed by an individualized constitutional treatment plan.

How does an online homeopathy consultation work?

You book a slot, then meet Dr. Libin Job over a scheduled video call. In homeopathy the consultation itself is the core of treatment — a long, detailed case-taking covering your symptoms, history, triggers and lifestyle. You then receive a personalized remedy plan, with remedies couriered or sourced locally, and structured follow-ups.

Is an online consultation as effective as visiting the clinic in person?

For homeopathy, the heart of the work is the in-depth interview rather than a physical exam, so a thorough video consultation captures the same detailed history that guides remedy selection. Many of our autoimmune and allergy patients are treated entirely online with regular follow-up.

What autoimmune and allergic conditions do you treat online?

We focus on autoimmune and allergic disease — including Hashimoto's thyroiditis, chronic spontaneous urticaria, dermographism, mast cell activation (MCAS), eczema and eosinophilic esophagitis — using a root-cause, constitutional approach rather than symptom suppression alone.

How will I receive my remedies if I live abroad?

Depending on your country, remedies are either couriered to you or matched to equivalents you can source locally, and we guide you through it after your consultation. Dosage and schedule are explained clearly so you can follow the plan from anywhere.

Do I need to stop my current medication to start homeopathy?

No. Never stop or change prescribed medication on your own. Our constitutional approach is designed to work alongside your conventional care, and any changes to existing treatment should always be made with your prescribing doctor.

How long before I see results?

It depends on the condition, how long it has been present and individual factors, so we avoid blanket promises. Chronic autoimmune and allergic conditions are addressed gradually at the root level, and your follow-up schedule is built around tracking real, measurable change over time.

What happens in the first consultation and how long does it take?

The first session is the longest, because a complete picture is what makes constitutional treatment work. Dr. Libin Job explores your full history, triggers, and emotional and physical patterns, then explains your individualized plan and next steps.

Can homeopathy work alongside my conventional autoimmune or allergy treatment?

Yes. We frame constitutional homeopathy as root-cause and complementary, not a replacement for necessary conventional care, and we coordinate around your existing treatment so the two work together rather than in conflict.

What languages and time zones do you work across?

Consultations are conducted in English and Malayalam, scheduled to suit international time zones across the Gulf, UK, Europe, North America and South Asia. You receive a confirmed appointment time in your own local time.

Is my online consultation private and confidential?

Yes. Consultations are one-to-one with Dr. Libin Job over a secure video link, and your medical information is kept confidential.
